We are searching for Translators!

Encrypt reaches the steam store on 1.6.2020.
From that date on we will also start the translation project for encrypt!

The search phase for translators starts now! To sign up, join our Discord Server,
simply go to the #encrypt-be-a-translator (encrypt-be-a-translator) channel,
specify the language you want to translate the game into and wait for a reply from one of the Developers!
